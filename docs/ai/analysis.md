Get AI-powered feedback and improvement suggestions for your presentations.

## Overview

DreamBot can analyze your presentation and provide suggestions for improvement, delivered as comments on your slides. It's like having a presentation coach review your work.

![AI Analysis dialog explaining that AI will review slides and suggest improvements for clarity, structure, and visual balance](/images/screenshots/ai-analysis-dialog.png)

## How It Works

DreamBot reviews your presentation and generates actionable suggestions:

- **Content improvements** - Clearer wording, better explanations
- **Structure recommendations** - Slide order, flow improvements
- **Design suggestions** - Visual hierarchy, readability
- **Clarity enhancements** - Simplify complex ideas
- **Engagement tips** - Make content more compelling

## Triggering Analysis

![DreamBot analysis dialog](/images/screenshots/ai-dreambot-dialog.png)

### Analyze Full Presentation

1. Open your presentation
2. Click the **✨ DreamBot** button in the toolbar
3. Wait for analysis to complete
4. Review suggestions in comments

### Analyze Single Slide

1. Select a slide
2. Click **AI** > **Analyze This Slide**
3. Get focused feedback on that slide

### Automatic Analysis

Some features can be enabled for automatic analysis:
- On publish (suggests improvements before going live)
- On share (ensures quality before collaboration)

## Analysis Options

### Focus Areas

Choose what DreamBot should focus on:

| Focus | What It Analyzes |
|-------|------------------|
| **Content** | Text quality, accuracy, completeness |
| **Structure** | Flow, organization, logical order |
| **Clarity** | Readability, jargon, complexity |
| **Engagement** | Interest, calls to action, storytelling |
| **All** | Comprehensive review |

### Depth

- **Quick** - High-level observations
- **Standard** - Balanced detail
- **Deep** - Thorough slide-by-slide analysis

## Reviewing Suggestions

Suggestions appear as comments on relevant slides.

### Finding Suggestions

1. Open the comment panel
2. Filter by **DreamBot** or **AI Suggestions**
3. Review suggestions in context

### Suggestion Format

Each suggestion includes:
- **Issue** - What could be improved
- **Recommendation** - Specific suggestion
- **Rationale** - Why this matters

Example:
> **Issue:** This slide has too much text
> **Recommendation:** Break this into 2-3 slides or use bullet points
> **Rationale:** Slides with less text are easier to read and more memorable

### Suggestion Types

Look for these indicators:
- **Quick fix** - Apply with one click
- **Needs review** - Requires your judgment
- **Consider** - Optional improvement

## Acting on Suggestions

### Quick Fixes

For suggestions with one-click fixes:
1. Click **Apply** on the suggestion
2. Change is made automatically
3. Suggestion is resolved

### Manual Changes

For suggestions requiring your input:
1. Review the recommendation
2. Make changes in the editor
3. Click **Resolve** on the suggestion

### Dismissing Suggestions

If a suggestion doesn't apply:
1. Click **Dismiss**
2. Optionally add a note
3. Suggestion is hidden

## Types of Feedback

### Content Quality

- Unclear or ambiguous statements
- Missing context or explanations
- Unsupported claims
- Jargon that needs definition

### Structure and Flow

- Slides that should be reordered
- Missing introduction or conclusion
- Abrupt topic transitions
- Repetitive content

### Visual Clarity

- Too much text on a slide
- Poor visual hierarchy
- Inconsistent formatting
- Accessibility issues

### Engagement

- Opportunities for questions
- Places to add examples
- Calls to action
- Story elements

## Best Practices

### When to Analyze

Good times to run analysis:
- After first draft is complete
- Before sharing for review
- Before publishing
- When feeling stuck

### Using Suggestions Effectively

- Don't accept everything blindly
- Consider your audience and context
- Use suggestions as a starting point
- Trust your judgment for tone and style

### Iterative Improvement

1. Run analysis
2. Address major issues
3. Run again if needed
4. Fine-tune manually

## Privacy

### What DreamBot Sees

To analyze your presentation, content is sent to the AI:
- All slide text
- Speaker notes
- Presentation structure

### What's Not Sent

- Images (unless alt text analysis)
- Your personal information
- Collaborator details

### Data Handling

Content is processed but not stored by AI providers. See [AI Setup](/docs/ai/getting-started/) for privacy details.
