# Slides Panel

The slides panel on the left side of the editor is your command center for managing slides.

![Editor with slides panel showing thumbnails and navigation](/images/screenshots/slides-panel.png)

## Navigating Slides

- **Click** a slide thumbnail to select and edit it
- **Arrow keys** move through the list when the panel is focused
- The selected slide shows in the edit and preview panels

## Adding Slides

- **+ Slide** button at the top adds a new slide at the end
- **Insert between** - Hover between slides to reveal an insert button
- Choose a [slide type](/docs/slide-types/) when adding

## Reordering Slides

**Drag and drop** slides to reorder:

1. Click and hold a slide thumbnail
2. Drag to the new position
3. Release to drop

## Nesting Slides

Create hierarchy by nesting slides under others:

- Drag a slide slightly to the right to nest it under the slide above
- Nested slides appear indented
- Parent slides can be collapsed to hide their children
- Useful for organizing sections or creating "appendix" slides

## Hiding Slides

Hide slides without deleting them:

- Click the visibility icon on a slide
- Hidden slides are skipped during presentation
- Useful for backup slides or work-in-progress content

## Deleting Slides

- Click the delete icon on a slide
- Confirm the deletion
- Deleted slides cannot be recovered (duplicate your presentation first if unsure)

## Slide Thumbnails

Thumbnails show a miniature preview of each slide. They update as you edit.
