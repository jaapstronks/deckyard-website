Start a fresh presentation and build it slide by slide.

## Creating a New Presentation

![New presentation dialog with Start blank, From template, and From content options, theme selection, and language toggle](/images/screenshots/new-presentation-dialog.png)

1. From the presentation list (`/app`), click **New Presentation**
2. Enter a **title** for your presentation
3. Select the **language** (NL or EN) - this sets the primary editing language
4. Click **Create**

You'll be taken directly to the editor in your chosen language.

## Choosing a Theme

When creating a new presentation, you can select from available themes that define:

- Color palette
- Typography
- Default layouts
- Logo placement

The theme is applied automatically to all slides.

## Adding Your First Slides

Once in the editor:

1. Click **+ Slide** to add a new slide
2. Choose a [slide type](/docs/slide-types/) that fits your content
3. Fill in the fields for that slide type
4. The preview updates live as you type

![Slide type picker showing available slide types by category](/images/screenshots/slide-type-picker-new.png)

## Tips

- **Start with a title slide** - Most presentations begin with a title slide that introduces the topic
- **Plan your structure** - Consider adding section slides to break up your content
- **Write speaker notes early** - Add notes while the content is fresh in your mind
