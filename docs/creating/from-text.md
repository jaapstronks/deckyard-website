# Create from Text

Use the AI wizard to generate a presentation from raw text - perfect for turning documents, notes, or briefs into slides.

## How It Works

1. Open the **AI Wizard** from the presentation list
2. Paste your text (raw notes, document content, meeting notes - any text works)
3. Choose the output language (NL or EN)
4. Click **Generate**

The AI analyzes your content and creates slides using appropriate [slide types](/docs/slide-types/).

## What the AI Does

- Identifies key points and structures them into slides
- Chooses appropriate slide types (title, content, lists, quotes, etc.)
- Preserves the logical flow of your content
- Creates speaker notes where appropriate

## After Generation

Generated presentations are starting points. You should:

1. **Review each slide** - Check that the AI captured your intent
2. **Adjust headings** - Make titles more engaging
3. **Add images** - Replace placeholders with actual images
4. **Write speaker notes** - Add personal notes for presenting
5. **Translate** - Generate the second language version if needed

## Tips for Better Results

- **More context = better output** - Provide detailed text for best results
- **Structure helps** - Text with clear headings generates better slides
- **Iterate** - You can always add more AI-generated slides later via "AI Add..." in the editor
