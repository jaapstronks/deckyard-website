# Live Preview

The preview panel shows how your slide will look when presented.

## Real-Time Updates

The preview updates **live** as you type. Every change you make is immediately reflected.

## Preview Panel

The right column of the editor shows the current slide rendered exactly as it will appear in presentation mode.

![Editor with the preview panel on the right showing the rendered slide, comment input, and presenter notes](/images/screenshots/editor-full-context.png)

## Lightbox View

Click the **magnifying glass** icon (or click the preview thumbnail) to open the slide in a larger lightbox view. This is useful for:

- Reviewing your deck slide-by-slide at near-presentation size
- Checking details on complex slides
- Reviewing image placement and positioned comments

### Navigating Slides

You can browse through your entire presentation without leaving the lightbox:

- **Arrow keys** — Press `←` / `→` to move to the previous or next slide
- **Navigation buttons** — Use the prev/next buttons in the header
- **Slide counter** — Shows your current position (e.g. "3 / 12")

The slides panel and editor form stay in sync — when you navigate in the lightbox, the selected slide updates everywhere. When you close the lightbox, you'll be on the slide you last viewed.

### Positioned Comments

If comments are enabled, positioned comment markers are visible on the slide. Click a marker to view its thread, or use the **pin** button to place a new comment on a specific spot.

### Closing

Press **Escape** or click outside the slide to close the lightbox. If a comment popup is open, Escape closes the popup first.

## Preview vs. Presentation

The preview shows:
- Final rendered appearance
- Applied theme styles
- Image positioning

The preview does not show:
- Animations and transitions (these play in presentation mode)
- Step-by-step reveals
- Interactive elements (polls, etc.)

## Language Preview

The preview shows the current editing language. Switch languages at the top of the editor to preview the other version.
