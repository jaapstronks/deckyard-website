# Trash & Recovery

Recover accidentally deleted presentations from the trash.

## How Trash Works

When you delete a presentation, it moves to trash instead of being permanently deleted. This gives you a safety net.

## Moving to Trash

1. Open the presentation menu (three dots)
2. Click **Delete**
3. Confirm the deletion
4. The presentation moves to trash

## Viewing Trash

![Trash view showing deleted presentations with restore options](/images/screenshots/trash-view.png)

1. Go to the presentation list
2. Click **Trash** in the sidebar or filter
3. See all trashed presentations

## Restoring Presentations

From the trash view:

1. Find the presentation to restore
2. Click **Restore**
3. The presentation returns to your main list

All content, slides, and settings are preserved.

## Permanent Deletion

To permanently delete a presentation:

1. Go to trash
2. Click **Delete Permanently** on the presentation
3. Confirm - this cannot be undone

## Automatic Cleanup

Trashed presentations may be automatically deleted after a retention period (configured by your admin).

## Tips

- Use trash as a holding area for presentations you might need later
- Check trash before assuming content is lost
- For important presentations, consider [duplicating](/docs/creating/duplicating) before making major changes
