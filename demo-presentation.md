## Slide 2: The Problem

Creating great presentations today means choosing between:

- **PowerPoint/Google Slides** — No AI, no audience interaction
- **Mentimeter/Slido** — Great polls, but you can't build slides
- **Beautiful.ai/Gamma** — Nice AI, but locked in, US-hosted, credit limits
- **Claper** — Upload-only, no editor
- **Presenton** — Generate-only, no live features

You're forced to juggle multiple tools. And none of them let you own your data.

---

## Slide 3: Enter Deckyard

The complete package:

- Full slide editor with 44 slide types
- AI-powered deck generation
- Live audience interaction built-in
- Self-host or EU cloud
- Open source (MIT license)

Finally, one tool that does it all.

## Slide 4: 44 Slide Types

Build anything you need:

- **Data** — Charts, graphs, progress visualizations
- **Media** — Video embeds, image galleries, maps
- **Structure** — Titles, sections, timelines, cards
- **Interactive** — Polls, Q&A, forms

Plus 5 transition types and speaker notes on every slide.

- **Parallel processing** — Generate sections simultaneously for speed
- **Transform slides** — AI helps convert between slide types

Your AI, your costs, your control.
